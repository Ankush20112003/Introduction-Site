About College:

Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra (KUK) is a university established on 11 January 1956 in Kurukshetra, in the Indian state of Haryana. Spread over 473 acres (1.91 km), the KUK campus is located on the western bank of Brahma Sarovar in the Hindu holy city of Kurukshetra.
